From laboratories to text editors


Prior to learning about web development, I graduated with a major in biology in 2016 and worked in leading liquid biopsy laboratories for the prenatal and cancer fields.

While working as a Research Associate in 2017, I was introduced to the concept of improving healthcare quality through web development and I immediately jumped into this movement.

In addition to participating in numerous hackathons to understand code writing and to contribute in the development of products, I have since signed up for a Udacity Nanodegree for Full Stack Web Developer and volunteered as part of the Silicon Valley Artificial Intelligence core team, a non-profit organization that promotes artificial intelligence in healthcare.

I am available for a full-time role in web development.
